Holding-Tabs

Holding tabs are small bridges for holding the milled workpiece.


  • Use Taps:
    activates Holding-Tab's
  • Depth
    depth of the upper edge of the tab
  • Length
    length of the tabs
  • Triangle-Type
    Shape of the tabs, rectangle or triangle
  • X-Grid
    Grid on which the tabs are set (X direction)
  • Y-Grid
    Grid on which the tabs are set (Y direction)
